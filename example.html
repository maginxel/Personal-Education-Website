In my journey as an aspiring Computer Science student, I developed a mobile translation app that converts English into four languages: French, Portuguese, Swedish, and Korean. This innovative project highlights my ability to build interactive applications that enhance communication and accessibility across cultures.

Purpose and functionality

Language barriers can make international communication difficult, whether in travel, education, or business. My translation app bridges this gap by allowing users to input English text and receive accurate translations in four different languages. Additionally, the app includes a voice output feature, which reads the translated text aloud in the selected language, ensuring clarity in pronunciation and comprehension.
